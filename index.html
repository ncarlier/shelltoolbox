shelltoolbox

Welcome to my shell toolbox.

Get the script:

$ curl -O ncarlier.github.io/shelltoolbox/echo

Run the script:

$ curl -s ncarlier.github.io/shelltoolbox/echo | sh

With arguments:

$ curl -s ncarlier.github.io/shelltoolbox/echo | bash -s -- arg1 arg2
